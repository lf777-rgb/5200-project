## Data hygiene notes

- Keep Tehran market data separate from national inflation data.
- Mark whether each number is a price level, year-over-year change, or monthly change.
- Do not over-claim precision where the source only gives approximate wording such as "about four times higher."
- Use absolute dates in the narrative: March 2025, October 2025, February 2026, March 2026.
